METRIC WHAT IT MEASURES WHAT IT SIGNALS TO OTHERS

[FOLLOWER COUNT] HOW MANY PEOPLE HAVE CHOSEN TO FOLLOW A PROFILE OVER TIME OVERALL SOCIAL STATUS, INFLUENCE, AND PERCEIVED IMPORTANCE WITHIN THE NETWORK

UNLIKE TRADITIONAL REPUTATION [WHICH IS BUILT
GRADUALLY THROUGH RELATIONSHIPS AND DIRECT INTERACTIONS]
